When placing an order the customer must ensure that their email address entered is correct and their mail box is in full working order so they are able to receive confirmation emails that are sent once their order has been accepted.

All products are subject to availability. If an order is placed and an item is not in stock then an email informing you of this will be sent and that item will be cancelled and refunded.
